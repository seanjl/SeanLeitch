Creation of Snowblock Media

21 Sep 2019

Throughout 2019 I've been learning web development, design and accessibility. After working on a few projects of my own, I decided it was time to use my knowledge to help others achieve their goals.

On October 1st, Snowblock Media will begin operations and start accepting projects. Our services will include website development, English/Spanish translations and mobile app development.

I aim to achieve the best possible results while maintaining a lower price than competitors thus allowing small and medium businesses to have a polished, professional website at a reasonable price

My goal is to constantly improve myself and develop my skills as much as I can in order to provide value to the world. I'm excited for the future and I'm confident Snowblock Media will one day be an empire.

-Sean Leitch, CEO of Snowblock Media
