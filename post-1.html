Welcome to my blog

18 Jul 2019

I just came back from a trip to Austria and Slovakia and now it's time to get back to the grind

I've deleted my old blog (seanleitchblog) and integrated it into my website (seanleitch). I'm also working on redesigning my website and snowblock's website.

The old designs had too many colors on the cards and a high text to info ratio. This new design is clean, easy to use, and doesn't saturate the viewers' eyes.

On my website I've decided to go for a nice, clean, purple and lilac pallette, which is visually satisfying.

On the snowblock website I've swapped out the android green for a cool blue hue.


This first post is basically just a test to see how the blog will look like, and to explain what it's going to be about.

I'll be writing articles about web development, technology, personal development and my personal opinions on any subject that comes to mind.

That's it for the first post, I'm going to end it with a pic of my trip to Austria, what a beautiful country!
