Editing a Music Video

8 Aug 2019

I've got two close friends who are rappers and a couple weeks ago I recorded some footage for them to make a music video.

I spent last week in NYC and as soon as I got home I went straight to work and started editing the video.The video will be released tommorow (August 9th) on YouTube and will be included on my portfolio.

I'm pretty proud of what I/we created considering the low-budget of this project and the little experience I have in Adobe Premiere Pro. Even though I've worked with other video-editing software in the past, this will be my first published project created with Premiere Pro.

I'm excited for what the future holds, and I'm going to invest more time into developing my editing skills with this app.


Here's the video, I hope to improve my abilities and produce better videos.
